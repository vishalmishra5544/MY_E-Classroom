About "My E-Classroom"

"My E-Classroom" is a initiative towards the advancement of online learning in this generation. It is developed as a project by Vishal.Mishra, Kalpesh.Vishwakarma, Aniket.Mane present in department of computer engineering of Atharva College of Engineering.This projeect is motivated towards the betterment of E-Learning and expanding the borders to reach students in every part of the country through the medium of internet and other technologies.This project deals with faclilities like providing three types of users - Admin(school or college administrators),Teachers,Students.Task of Admin- maintain and manipulate the records of students,their profiles,courses(standards),subjects,lessons,attendance,assignments(practice exams). Task of Teacher - Create and update lessons and provide recorded video lectures,ppts,notes in the various subjects, take and maintain records of attendances, create and maintain records of assignments and students marks. Task of Student-access video lectures,ppts,notes of lessons in various subjects,mark and see attendance, view and take assignments(practice exams) and view marks.
